about me

I'm a PhD candidate in Applied Mathematics at the University of Waterloo, under the supervision of Dr. Chris Bauch and Dr. Madhur Anand. My research interests are roughly at the intersection of mathematical ecology, epidemiology and dynamical systems. I'm also more broadly interested in music, botany, ecology, and open source.

I do the majority of my research development in Julia these days, and vastly prefer working in Julia to other languages for scientific programming.

Code for my publications is hosted at my UW Gitlab account, please see the research section of my website for links to specific repositories.
